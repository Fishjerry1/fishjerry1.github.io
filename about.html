About Me

Hello! I'm Bassey Jeremiah Usang, a Data Analyst, Public Health Researcher, and Monitoring & Evaluation Specialist with a growing portfolio in data science, health informatics, and geospatial analytics. I hold Master's degree in Health Economics and have successfully contributed to several large-scale development projects funded by USAID, Global Fund, and other international agencies.

My data journey began as a Data Enumerator and evolved rapidly as I mastered tools such as Power BI, DHIS2, SQL, SPSS, STATA, Python, ArcGIS, and QGIS. I have delivered analytic insights and reports for health programs focused on HIV/AIDS, OVC, Maternal and Child Health, and routine immunization.

Over the years, I've worked with organizations such as Society for Family Health, GEOANALTECH LTD/GTE, and Centre for Clinical Care and Clinical Research Nigeria, where I led data quality assessments, field supervision, dashboard development, and strategic learning for program optimization. I currently serve as the Technical Lead for Training & Development at Geospatial Analytics Technologies (GEOANALTECH), where I support capacity-building programs and lead our PEPTalk Series and Data Bootcamps.
