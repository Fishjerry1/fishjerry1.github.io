Architecting Big Data Pipelines

Posted on July 8, 2025

From raw logs to analytics tables—here’s how to build robust pipelines:

  1. Ingest: Kafka, MQTT, or cloud streams.
  2. Process: Spark Streaming vs. Flink.
  3. Store: Data lakes (Parquet), warehouses (Redshift/BigQuery).
  4. Orchestrate: Airflow, Prefect, or Dagster.
