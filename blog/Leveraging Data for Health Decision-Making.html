Leveraging Data for Health Decision-Making

Posted on February 15, 2025

Data-driven decisions improve outcomes. Here’s how to build a decision‑support dashboard:

  1. Integrate multiple data sources (surveillance, surveys, administrative).
  2. Define key indicators and thresholds.
  3. Implement alerts and trigger rules in Power BI or DHIS2.
  4. Train users on interpreting trends and taking action.

Example: Malnutrition Response

When admission rates exceed 10%, trigger community screening campaigns.
