Building a Career in Data Analytics

Posted on March 5, 2025

Your roadmap from beginner to pro:

  1. Master Excel and SQL fundamentals.
  2. Learn a scripting language (Python/R).
  3. Explore visualization tools (Power BI/Tableau).
  4. Build a portfolio of real‑world projects.
  5. Engage with communities & mentor others.

Never stop learning. Data careers grow with your curiosity and intentional practice.
